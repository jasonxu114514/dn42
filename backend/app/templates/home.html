How to peer with us

  1. Log in with your dn42 ASN (Kioubit, or the Telegram bot).
  2. Open New Peer, pick a node, and paste your WireGuard public key. Your endpoint is optional — leave it blank and your side dials us.
  3. Choose your in-tunnel IP. The link-local default works out of the box; a ULA (fd00::/8) is also accepted.
  4. We validate and auto-deploy to the node. Open the peer to copy our endpoint, public key, and BGP neighbor address — then bring up your side.
